D-zone pressure (If we are out of possession)

Box Plus 1

We will create a box the with defense and wingers. The centre will chase the puck carrier.

  • The box will collapse on the house when threats are there.
  • The farside winger will provide support in front of the net, when needed.


Collapsing

All players will collapse on the puck carrier when they are in the box.


Farside Winger

The farside winger will provide support in front of the net, when needed.


Battle Mode (Loose/50-50)

If the puck is loose or in a 50-50 situation, the 2nd closest player will engage to improve our chances of winning.






D-Zone Transition To Breakout

If we gain control of the puck in the D-Zone we are in breakout mode
