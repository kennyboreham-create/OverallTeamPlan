In Possession

Focus on maintaining puck control through clean transitions, crisp tape-to-tape passing, and establishing strong offensive zone cycles. Utilize defensemen at the blue line to stretch the opposing coverage framework.

Out of Possession

Implement structured backchecking layers and a disciplined 1-2-2 neutral zone trap. Collapse down low to protect high-danger scoring areas in front of the net while maintaining active sticks to disrupt passing lanes.

Loose/50-50

Anticipate structural transitions during contested plays along the boards. Support the primary battler with a secondary close-support player to cleanly secure possession out of scrums and heavy physical tie-ups.

When to Attack

Exploit dynamic odd-man rushes on counter-attacks immediately following defensive takeaways. Drive deep past the dots when the opposing defense is caught flat-footed or stuck in an extended shift layout.

Communicate

Maintain consistent vocal cues for blind spot alerts, defensive switches, and line change pacing. Ensure clear point-to-point talk during high-pressure cycles to eliminate coverage confusion in the zone.
